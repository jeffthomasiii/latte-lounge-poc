Monday can carry a lot before it even begins.

In this Sacred Pause episode, we take a few quiet minutes to pray over the workweek, surrender the pressure to perform, and ask God for peace, wisdom, and steadiness before the responsibilities of the day begin.

## Episode Prayer

Lord, before this week begins moving quickly, help me pause with You.

Give me peace for what I cannot control, wisdom for what I need to handle, and grace for the people I will encounter.

Let me lead, work, serve, and respond from a place of peace instead of pressure.

Amen.
