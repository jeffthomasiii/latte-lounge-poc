---
title: "Prayer for Overwhelming Mornings"
subtitle: "A short prayer for the morning when your mind is already carrying too much."
---

Lord,

Before I rush into this day, help me pause.

You already see what is ahead of me. You know the meetings, the conversations, the responsibilities, and the quiet burdens I am carrying.

Anchor my heart in You.

Give me peace before pressure, wisdom before reaction, and grace for what this day requires.

Amen.
