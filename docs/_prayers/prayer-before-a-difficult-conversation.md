---
title: "Prayer Before a Difficult Conversation"
subtitle: "A short prayer for wisdom, peace, courage, and grace."
---

Lord,

I know this conversation may not be easy.

Help me enter it with humility instead of defensiveness, courage instead of fear, and wisdom instead of reaction.

Guard my tone.

Steady my emotions.

Help me listen well, speak truthfully, and honor You in both my words and my posture.

Give me peace before I speak, clarity while I listen, and grace for what I cannot control.

Amen.
